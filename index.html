About Me

Get to Know Me

I'm Yutian Qin, a passionate and driven software developer. Originally hailing from China, I have made my academic journey international, currently pursuing my master's degree in software development at the University of Utah.
Throughout my academic journey, I have sought opportunities to bridge the gap between design and technology. I believe that the combination of my industrial design background and software development expertise allows me to approach projects with a unique perspective, emphasizing seamless user experiences and efficient technical implementations.
